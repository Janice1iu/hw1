Chihara Junior

About

Chihara Junior (千原ジュニア) is a Japanese comedian, MC and actor. He is the brain of the comedy duo Chihara Brothers. Born in Fukuchiyama, Kyoto on 30 March 1974, Junior moved to Osaka and joined Yoshimoto Kogyo in 1989. He is active on TV and stage for over 30 years.
