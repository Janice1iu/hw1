About

Chihara Junior (千原ジュニア) is a Japanese comedian, MC and actor. He is the brain of the comedy duo Chihara Brothers.

Chihara Junior is famous for his one-man talk show and surrealistic stage comedy.

Born in Fukuchiyama, Kyoto on 30 March 1974, Junior moved to Osaka and formed the duo with his older bother in 1989. As a Yoshimoto comedian, he has been active on TV and stage for over 30 years. He also wrote and published several novels, comics and haiku collections.
