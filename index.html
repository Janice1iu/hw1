About

Chihara Junior (千原ジュニア) is a Japanese comedian, MC and actor. He is the brain of the comedy duo Chihara Brothers.

Chihara Junior is famous for his one-man talk show and surrealistic stage comedy.

Born in Fukuchiyama, Kyoto on 30 March 1974, Junior moved to Osaka and joined Yoshimoto Kogyo in 1989. He has been active on TV and stage for over 30 years. He also wrote and published several novels, comics and haiku collections.
